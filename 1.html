About Us

We are the Environmental Service in Akkar, dedicated to preserving the environment, promoting cleanliness, and caring for animals in our community.

Our Activities

We organize regular clean-up drives, tree planting events, and educational workshops to raise awareness about environmental issues.

Environmental Cleanliness

We believe in keeping our surroundings clean and free from pollution. Join us in our efforts to make Akkar a cleaner and greener place!

Animal Care

We advocate for the welfare of animals and provide support for stray and injured animals in our region.
